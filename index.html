Google Fonts is a library of over 800 libre licensed fonts and serves over 135 languages worldwide. Chinese typeface design and font development presents many challenges, from the time needed to create the huge character set to the web infrastructure required to serve the massive font files. Typographic Calendar is designed and created with libre web fonts. We hope to invite designers and developers to experiment with the fonts on Early Access as the standards and technology continue to develop.
—Project made by Yuin Chien.
