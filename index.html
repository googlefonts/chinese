Google Fonts是一個擁有超過八百個開源字體的字庫。任在技術研發階段的字體，會透過Early Access預先發表。中文字體設計需要大量字符，網頁字體也需要特殊的科技來下載龐大的檔案。因此，在推動技術研發的同時，我們也邀清設計人與工程師供相盛舉，來試用Early Access的中文字體。

Google Fonts is a library of over 800 libre licensed fonts. Early Access is a showcase for fonts that aren't yet fully supported. Chinese typeface design and font development presents many challenges, from the huge character set to the web infrastructure required to serve the font files. As standards and technology continue to develop, we invite designers and developers to play with the Chinese fonts on Early Access.

This website is created by Yuin Chien.
